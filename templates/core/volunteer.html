VOLUNTEER WITH UNMUTE INDIA

Make room for a student to be heard.

Volunteers help with careful peer listening, community moderation and responsible student advocacy. This is not a counselling role, and applying does not provide immediate access to private messages.

See how to apply ↓

WHAT VOLUNTEERS MAY HELP WITH

Different skills.
One careful standard.

01

Peer listening

Respond with empathy, avoid diagnosis and ask before offering advice.

02

Community moderation

Help keep public stories and responses supportive, private and respectful.

03

Research & accountability

Organise sources, timelines and student concerns without overstating claims.

04

Platform support

Contribute writing, design, outreach or operational skills to the mission.

BEFORE YOU APPLY

Care requires boundaries.

You do not need to have all the answers. You do need patience, discretion and the willingness to follow a reviewed process.

  • Protect privacyNever copy, share or discuss private student content outside authorised workflows.
  • Stay within the roleDo not present yourself as a counsellor, doctor or legal professional unless separately verified.
  • Listen without judgementRespect identity, language, pauses and the student's right not to continue.
  • Accept review and trainingApplications are screened; access is role-based and never automatic.
