HOW PUBLIC SHARING WORKS

Private first. Public only by choice.

A student shares privately, chooses whether public review is allowed, and staff remove identifying details before approving anything. Consent can later be withdrawn with the private tracking code.

Explicit consentPrivacy reviewStaff moderationRemoval available

MODERATION & PRIVACY

Public does not mean unprotected.

  • Every public voice is reviewed
  • Personal details are removed
  • Comments wait for moderation
  • Support—not popularity—is centred
  • Unsafe responses can be reported
  • Owners can withdraw consent
