REAL PEOPLE ARE LISTENING

Not a bot. Not a call centre. Real people who care.

Your message is reviewed by an authorised Unmute India member. They are expected to listen without judgement, protect your privacy and avoid forcing advice.

  • We listen before advising
  • We do not shame or compare
  • We respect anonymous sharing
  • We do not contact you outside the platform without permission
  • We do not pretend every volunteer is a counsellor
  • Serious concerns are handled by authorised senior members

Volunteers provide peer listening and platform support. Professional labels appear only after real credentials have been reviewed.

What does private mean here?

Your submission enters a restricted support workflow. It is not placed in Unmuted Voices automatically. Authorised members receive access according to their role, and public use requires a separate clear choice plus moderation. Production media should use private storage and expiring access links.
