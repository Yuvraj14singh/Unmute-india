COMMUNITY STANDARDS

Care needs more than good intentions.

These standards protect private student voice, public conversations and evidence-led accountability across Unmute India.

ListeningPrivacyPublic contentAccountability

THE SHARED PROMISE

Listen before advising.
Protect before publishing.
Verify before claiming.

Everyone using or supporting this platform shares responsibility for student dignity.

Students may arrive without perfect words. Volunteers may not have every answer. Public discussions may involve difficult issues. What matters is that every action stays respectful, privacy-aware and honest about its limits.

01

PRIVATE LISTENING

Respond like a person—not an authority.

Do

Listen without judgement

Acknowledge what the student shared and let them choose how much to say.

Do

Ask before advising

Support may mean listening, helping with words or discussing one practical next step.

Avoid

Diagnosis and certainty

Do not diagnose, prescribe, interrogate or make promises you cannot keep.

Avoid

Pressure to continue

A student can pause, leave or decline advice at any time.

PRIVACY

A private message stays private.

Submitting something for support does not make it a public story. Public sharing is a separate choice with separate review.

  1. 01

    Do not reveal identity

    Never expose names, contact details, institutions, locations or identifying context without a valid reason and clear permission.

  2. 02

    Use authorised workflows

    Private content must not be copied into personal messages, screenshots, outside documents or informal groups.

  3. 03

    Respect consent withdrawal

    When public-sharing consent is withdrawn, the public copy must be removed while protected records follow platform policy.

02

UNMUTED VOICES & COMMENTS

Public support must remain safe to receive.

Welcome here

  • Supportive, relevant responses
  • Respectful disagreement without attacking a person
  • Experience shared without claiming it applies to everyone
  • Gentle encouragement and practical suggestions when invited

Removed or restricted

  • Harassment, hate, threats or humiliation
  • Private or identifying information
  • Unsafe instructions, diagnosis or coercive advice
  • Spam, impersonation or repeated duplicate comments
  • Content encouraging violence or self-harm

EVIDENCE & ACCOUNTABILITY

A demand can be urgent without turning an allegation into a fact.

01

Show the source

Use accessible source links, dates, document context and verification status wherever possible.

02

Separate fact and claim

Describe allegations, demands, official records and opinions accurately.

03

Stay peaceful

No threats, targeted abuse, doxxing or encouragement of unlawful harm.

04

Correct the record

Update or remove material when credible new evidence changes the picture.

VOLUNTEER BOUNDARIES

Access is earned, limited and reviewable.

Volunteer applications are reviewed before any role is assigned. Private access is never automatic and should be limited to what the role genuinely requires.

  • Follow training and moderation decisions
  • Use only the permissions assigned to your role
  • Disclose conflicts or situations beyond your ability
  • Escalate safety concerns through the platform workflow
  • Never claim unverified professional qualifications

WHEN SOMETHING BREAKS THESE RULES

Report it. Do not amplify it.

Use the report option on public comments or contact Unmute India with the relevant link and a short explanation. Reports are reviewed; reporting does not guarantee a specific outcome.
