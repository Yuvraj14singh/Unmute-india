PRACTICAL STUDENT SUPPORT

Clear information, one topic at a time.

Raise an exam grievance

Use the institution’s official channel, keep the acknowledgement number, save dated copies and state the remedy you are requesting.

Preserve screenshots and evidence

Keep original files, full URLs, dates and context. Avoid editing originals. Store a secure copy.

Write a formal complaint

Describe what happened in order, attach relevant proof, avoid speculation and request a clear time-bound response.

Ask for academic support

Contact a trusted teacher or official office, explain the immediate barrier and make one specific request such as a break, extension or meeting.

General information only. It is not individual legal or professional advice.
